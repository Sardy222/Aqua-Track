Water Usage Monitor - How to Use

Welcome to the Water Usage Monitor!

This guide will help you understand how to use the app efficiently.

📊 Dashboard

The dashboard displays the real-time water usage data.

  • 💧 Water Meter: Shows the current water consumption in cubic meters (m³).
  • ⏳ Flow Rate (L/m): Indicates the water flow rate in liters per minute.
  • 🔋 Battery Indicator: Displays the battery percentage of the device.
  • 📶 WiFi Strength: Indicates the signal strength of the connected network.
  • 📸 Manual Input: Click the camera or text icon to manually update the meter.

📈 Statistics

The statistics page provides detailed insights into your water consumption.

  • 📅 Daily Usage: Displays the amount of water consumed each day.
  • 📊 Monthly Trends: Helps track your water usage trends over time.
  • 🔎 Comparison Data: Compare your consumption with previous months.

🔧 Sidebar Menu

The sidebar provides additional settings and navigation options.

  • 👤 Sign Up / Log In: Create an account or log in to sync your data.
  • 🔗 Connect: Link your ESP32 device to the monitoring system.
  • 🔔 Notifications: View alerts for abnormal water usage or low battery.
  • ❓ Help & Support: Access FAQs and customer support.
  • ℹ️ About: View app details, terms of service, and privacy policy.
